Gauss Game

What is Gauss Game?

In a number guessing game, the user guesses a randomly generated secret number within a given number of attempts. After each guess, the user gets hints on whether their guess is too high, too low, or correct.

Rules

1. With a family member, take turns thinking of a number from 1 to 10. One person thinks of the number, and the other person has to guess the number.
2. After the first person gives the number, the second guest will get five chances.
3. If the second person is not able to guess, it goes to the third person.
4. If the third person guesses then the third person wins and if they fail to guess then the first person wins.
